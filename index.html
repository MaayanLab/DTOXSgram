DTOXSgram

The clustergram visualizes the top 250 up- and down-regulated genes for each drug-treated cell line. Up- or down-regulated gene expression is shown as red or blue tiles respectively in the matrix.
Zoom into the clustergram using scroll and reset zoom by double-clicking. Reorder using the reorder buttons or by double-clicking rows or column labels. Change the size of row/colum groups by moving the sliders.
